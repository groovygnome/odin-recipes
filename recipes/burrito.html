Guacamole

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced

Steps

  1. Mash avocado in a bowl.
  2. Stir in onion tomato and garlic
  3. Season with lim juice, salt, and pepper
  4. Cover and chill to allow flavors to blend
